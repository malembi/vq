About Us

Vision Quest is a dynamic consulting firm dedicated to driving positive change in the East and Horn of Africa. Our operational reach spans across multiple countries, including Kenya, Somalia, Sudan, South Sudan, Ethiopia, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda. By strategically partnering with government bodies, NGOs, and the private sector, we tackle complex challenges to enhance citizen welfare and foster sustainable development

why Vision Quest?

Our approach is anchored in evidence-based solutions, transforming the way we address challenges.

By strategically partnering with government bodies, NGOs, and the private sector, we tackle complex challenges to enhance citizen welfare and foster sustainable development. Our journey is one of helping to measure and tell the story of impactful change and meaningful transformation across various interventions and sectors in Africa.

We are grounded on local knowledge about communities, public and private sectors, as well as emerging trends and technologies to solve current challenges and anticipate the future. We are inspired by a vision to improve the welfare of individuals, communities and whole societies. Our operational reach spans across multiple countries, including Kenya, Somalia, Sudan, South Sudan, Ethiopia, Uganda, Tanzania, and Rwanda.

Our Vision

To be the epitome of exemplary service provision and to be a catalyst for measurable impact, envisioning a region where data and learning drive tangible, positive outcomes that leads to transformational change.

Our Mission

To provide clear, goal-oriented consulting services that empower our partners to overcome challenges, achieve milestones, and contribute to the sustained development of the communities we serve by virtue of our integrity, professionalism, efficiency and dedication.

Our Principles

At Vision Quest, our approach is guided by a set of core principles that define our methodology and commitment. These six principles form the bedrock of our mission to drive positive change:

  • Simplifying Complex Systems.
  • Illuminating Insights into Evidence.
  • Applying Design Thinking Principles.
  • Client-Focused, Apolitical, and Bold with Evidence.
  • Emboldening Partnerships with Integrity.
  • Celebrating Impactful Outcomes.
